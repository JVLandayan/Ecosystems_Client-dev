The History of Eco Tigers

  • 2009-2010

    The Team was founded in the year 2009 in preparation for the first ever Shell Eco Marathon Asia held at the Sepang F1 racetrack in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia from July 8-10, 2010. The first batch of Eco Tigers led by team leader Tim Orville produced the first T400 prototype vehicle which entered the said competition.

  • 2014-2016

    The Shell Eco Marathon Asia was held in Manila, Philippines with the track running around Luneta Park. UST Eco Tigers participated in the said event and started to join in producing the Urban Vehicle Concept in 2015.

  • 2018

    The Team switched to Battery-electric energy category for its Urban Class Vehicle Concept in time for the Shell Eco Marathon held in Singapore
